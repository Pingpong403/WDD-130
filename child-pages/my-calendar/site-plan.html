Purpose

If youâ€™ve ever had a crazy idea but not enough resources or motivation to put it in motion, then you know how frustrating it is to then forget what that idea was when the right moment hits; months later you have free time and a paycheck burning a hole in your pocket, but no ideas come to mind. What if there was a website that could keep track of your ideas and alert you when you have enough time/money to execute them?

Audience

Anyone who has varying amounts of free time and/or money. This could be anyone with a job, anyone attending school, anyone doing both at the same time, etc. I believe that truly anyone can use this website and it will be valuable. It could be a new companion app for someone who perfectly fits my description, or a useful tool for spontaneous people, or just a fun, new thing for hipsters. It doesn't need to be the most useful thing, in other words.
